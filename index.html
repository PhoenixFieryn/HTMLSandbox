Symbols and Entities

HTML can display various symbols and entities such as: © ™ ® ♦. They follow unicode encoding and can be represented by their names or encoding numbers. It's safer to escape special reserved characters in HTML such as "<" than to type it out directly. Be careful when decoding encoded characters as such operations can expose your website to XSS attacks!

HTML Semantics

HTML semantics are special tags that indicate what the content inside is supposed to be.

They aren't mandatory and their usage is subjective, or follows a specific guideline set by a particular project. Semantics include: nav, header, footer, aside, figure, figcaption, main, details, article, summary, etc.
